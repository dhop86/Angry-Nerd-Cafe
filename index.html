Nerd Coffee?

We're a bit nerdy here at Angry Nerd Cafe, Tampa's premier coffee shop for comic book enthusiasts, gamers, movie buffs, cosplayers, and of course—coffee lovers. All are welcome. Nerds are preferred.

Relax and get wired up on our lovingly brewed coffees. We proudly roast in house, using only the finest quality, fair-trade and fair-wage coffee beans sourced from organic growers.

Enjoy our complementary, lightning fast wifi as you savor your favorite brew. Bring your laptop to join friends and patrons in your favorite video game as you savor the best coffee in town!

Join us weekly for game and movie events. We have events multiple days per week, so don't miss out. See our Events page for details.

We believe we have the best customers any coffee shop could ever ask for, and we're proud reward our loyal fan base with exclusive deals. Consider joining our loyalty program to earn points and gain access to members only specials. Ask your barista for details today.

You've Got Questions—We've Got Answers


Can you accommodate for dairy allergies?

Absolutely, in fact we're proud to offer many plant based milk options including soy, oat, cashew, almond, and coconut milks.

Where do you source your coffee from?

Our coffees come from all over the world. We believe in ethical coffee, so we only purchase supplies from coffee growers that are both fair wage and fair trade. We also believe in a clean product, so we only serve organically grown coffee.

How does your rewards program work?

We love to show our appreciation to our loyal patrons. With our rewards program each beverage purchased earns you one point, and for every ten points you will earn a free drink of your choice of any size. Points are tracked using your phone number. Speak with your barista to register in our rewards program.

Do you have any games that are appropriate for children?

We're big on promoting a welcoming, family enviournment, and we offer many games that can be played and enjoyed by children. The youngest suggested age on many of our games is 8+. Feel free to bring your own games if you have younger children.

Are you pet friendly?

While we love our pets just as much as you, we respectfully request for you to leave your pets at home. We recognize that not everyone shares our love for animals, and we want to ensure a comfortable and welcoming enviournment for all of our patrons.

Where can I learn more about the nutritional information of your drinks?

Great question! We're health conscious as well, and believe in full transparency when it comes to what's in our product. In store we have nutrition information and ingredient lists for all of our beverages. Ask your barista for a copy.

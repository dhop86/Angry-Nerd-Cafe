Weekly Events

Join our fun weekly events. Prizes awarded to event winners.


Munchkins Monday

Monday from 6pm—9pm

Try your luck in our weekly Munchkin tournament. Winners receive a $10 voucher. We have 5 Munckin games, each hosting up to 6 players. Openings are limited, so reserve your seat ahead of time by speaking with your barista.

Nerdy Trivia Night

Wednesday from 6pm—9pm

Trivia for all of your favorite syfy and fantasy movies, comics, and TV shows. No player limits, so all are welcome to join. Winners receive a $10 voucher, and a 10% discount for the week!

Coffee & Costumes

Friday from 6pm—9pm

Dress up in cosplay as your favorite fantasy characters. Patrons will vote on their favorite costumes. Winners will receive a $20 voucher and have their photo featured on our cosplay wall of fame.
